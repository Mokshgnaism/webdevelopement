ABOUT US

ELECTRONICS & IOT Club is a student-run organization/club of Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. The Electronics & IOT Club is a community of students who derive pleasure in creating innovative electronic projects that can impact various industries. Each member of this family is provided with technical, monetary, and informative aid besides collaborative learning opportunities.
